Nuevo Leon

Nuevo Leon, which official name is "Estado Libre y Soberano de Nuevo Leon" is one of the 32 states that conform Mexico. Its capital and most populous city is Monterrey which has the highest gross domestic product of all metropolitan areas in Mexico and is the 3rd most-populated.

Nuevo Leon's culture is a mixture of cultures among which the aboriginal, the Spanish and the American stand out, being famous for its fairs, norteña music and a great spot for meat eaters with cabrito served with charros.
